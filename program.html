Program #1: APT Webinar

View/Download PDF Flyer
Video call link: Google Meet
Youtube Live Streaming Link: Watch on YouTube
Speaker: K P Unnikrishnan, eNeuroLearn, USA & Anthikad AI Institute
Audience: Physics teachers and interested students across the Colleges and Universities in Kerala.

The resource person will introduce Physics teachers and students to potential research topics and projects in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. He will showcase projects derived from past and ongoing work, and suggest topics particularly valuable for MSc and PhD students pursuing research in AI/ML. The session will also highlight the Kerala AI Research and Learning Initiative (KAIRALI), aimed at fostering a vibrant ecosystem for AI research in Kerala. The webinar will conclude with an open discussion on mentoring final year MSc students in designing and developing research projects in AI/ML.

Mentoring 2025

KAIRALI Mentoring 2025 is a new initiative to support MSc and PhD students in Kerala interested in research projects in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. The program includes calls for participation, guidance from experienced mentors, and opportunities to collaborate on impactful research.
